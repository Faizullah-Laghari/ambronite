SHELF-LIFE

We generally only sell products that have their best before date at minimum three months ahead (most of the time even 6-12 months). If not, we will explicitly state it during the checkout process.

Your Ambronite will keep best in room temperature in a dry, dark place away from direct sunlight.

Standard shelf life for products is:
- Complete Meal Shake: 12 months from manufacturing.
- Balanced Meal Shake: 15 months from manufacturing.

After opening the Meal Shakes will last according to the shelf life indicated in the Tub lid / Pouch, given that you properly close them when not in use.

We recommend enjoying your Ambronite meal within the next two hours after mixing it with water.

The powder needs NO cold storage. The fridge and other cold storage environments have high humidity which will easily spoil the dry powder. Store your Ambronite powder in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.

NUTRITIONAL DETAILS

All vitamins and minerals in Ambronite come from food-based ingredients, not from supplements. You can find our ingredients and nutrition information here.

Vitamin B12 in Ambronite is in the form of cyanocobalamin and comes from nutritional yeast. Rather than a blend of yeast and vitamins, the yeast is fermented in a B12 rich media and the yeast takes up the B12 in its framework. Cyanocobalamin is a widely used, highly bioavailable form of Vitamin B12 that the body can easily use.

The powerful nutritional yeast is the source for Vitamin D, along with the nutritious super-algae chlorella. The vitamin D is in the form of ergocalciferol-D2.

Each Ambronite Complete Meal Shake contains a hefty amount of greens, veggies and berries, which are packed with nutrients like vitamins, beneficial phytonutrients and antioxidants. Green plant superfoods like spinach and nettle are also a great source of calcium and Vitamin K.

The wild bilberries, cranberries and sea-buckthorn berries are true nutritional powerhouses and full of beneficial nutrients, like vitamin C and antioxidants along with other vitamins and minerals. Bilberries are known to have exceptional health benefits, thanks to their anthocyanins and other health promoting compounds not found in other berries or foods. Sea-buckthorns are a rare and excellent source of vitamins and healthy Omega 7 fatty acids.

Vitamin A found in plants is in the form of beta-carotene. Beta-carotene is a pigment found in plants. In Ambronite Supermeal, micro-algae Spirulina and leafy greens are the sources of beta-carotene, which is converted in the body to vitamin A.

Flaxseed is the main source of omega-3 fatty acids in Ambronite Supermeal formula. They come in the form of Alpha-Linoleic Acid (ALA) which is the form of omega-3 found in plants. One Full Meal contains 800 mg of Omega-3s (ALA), and trace amount of EPA from algae. Read more about plant-based fats in our blog.

Protein is essential for life and a building block for our muscles and bodies. Ambronite’s protein is in a form that your body can optimally utilize, because is a complete protein source and contains all the amino acids you need to thrive.

In Ambronite v5 the main protein source is high grade oat protein, complemented with oats, almonds and spirulina. Ambronite is dairy free, plant based and vegan friendly.
